PacificStem

Breaking the socioeconomic barrier by building a STEM tradition

Our Mission

PacificSTEM seeks to impact academia and society by closing the gap between the academic and professional areas of STEM and bringing high quality STEM-accessibility to all communities while cultivating compassionate leadership.

People attend STEM conferences and festivals around the country and sometimes internationally. Unfortunately, this opportunity is not evenly distributed due to financial and physical restrictions.
